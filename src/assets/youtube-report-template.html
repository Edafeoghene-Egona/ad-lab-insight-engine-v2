Last 7 Days · Jun 15 – Jun 22

Over the last 7 days, numoya's YouTube video campaigns served 1.42M impressions and drove 457,056 views at a 32.2% view rate and $0.024 average CPV — spending $10,975 to generate 654 conversions ($28,234 value). We tested 8 creatives this week, of which 3 cleared our win threshold (37.5% win rate). The standout is "GoBrush PRO — 3-Second Transformation": a 68.2% hook rate and 30.1% completion drove 214 conversions at the account's lowest CPV ($0.018). Hook quality is the clearest dividing line between winners and losers — the bottom two creatives ("Features Walkthrough", "Static Promo") never cleared a 35% hook rate and should be retired. Priorities: scale the top 3 transformation/before-after angles, re-cut the mid-pack UGC creatives with a stronger first 3 seconds, and pause the two losing creatives to reallocate spend.
